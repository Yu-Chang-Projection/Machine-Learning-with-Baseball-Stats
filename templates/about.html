Motivation

We are enthusiastic about Fantasy baseball, so we want to find a better way to evaluate players before the season starts. Therefore, we came up with the idea of making our own projection system to improve our drafting strategy.

Goals

The long-term goal of our project is to create a projection model that is accurate in predicting players performance. Aside from stats, that should also include predicting breakouts and bust performances.

Methodology

In our project, we reference stats from Fangraphs and Statcast for projections. For different to-project stats, we use our function to select the best stat combo and feed them into a regression model to train. For example, we use various plate discipline stats for our K% projections. As for samples, we select players with over 400 plate appearances in a single season from 2015 to 2021.
